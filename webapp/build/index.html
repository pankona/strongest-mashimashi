PhraGen

Generate a phrase with 3 random words

Concept

  1. Choice 3 words from English dictionary to generate strong password or passphrase.
  2. For easy remember, 3 words are choose as "adjective" "adjective" "noun".
  3. Words that are too short (less than 5) or too long (more than 10) are excluded.
  4. Note that since a word (listed in dictionary) may include whitespace or hyphen, the generated phrase may look like more than 3 words.
